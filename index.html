How to play

Roll dice button: When you press this button, the game will sort a number from 1 to 6 for you.

The number ONE: When the game sort a number 1, the game will switch to other player and your current score (not save score) will reset.

Hold button: The hold button will add your current score to your total score and pass to the next player turn.

How to win: the first person who get 100 points, win the game!!
